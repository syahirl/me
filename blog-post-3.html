a moment to appreciate

i still remember the day back when i was 16 years old. i wrote on my report card that i wanted to go to Imperial College London, and alhamdulillah, here i am.

the transition was not kind, adapting here wasnt easy. there were multiple humbling experiences and plenty of ups and downs, but surviving to this day is reason enough for me to be proud of myself. sometimes we tend to see the glass as half empty, but ive realized how important it is to appreciate every little thing and small success in life. having a strong support system is also something im incredibly grateful for. my parents, siblings, friends, and having 'A' beside me will always be my reason why.

im looking forward to making them proud. im going to spend another two years here, and im really excited for what’s ahead. After all, im really really proud to call this wonderful university a home away from home…
